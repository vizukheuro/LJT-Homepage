**Authors**: Yuzhen Huang, Yuzhuo Bai, Zhihao Zhu, Junlei Zhang, Jinghan Zhang, Tangjun Su, Junteng Liu, Chuancheng Lv, Yikai Zhang, Jiayi Lei, Yao Fu, Maosong Sun, Junxian He

**Venue**: NeurIPS 2023

This paper introduces C-Eval, a comprehensive multi-level and multi-discipline Chinese evaluation suite for foundation models. C-Eval covers a wide range of subjects and difficulty levels, providing a robust benchmark for evaluating Chinese language models.
