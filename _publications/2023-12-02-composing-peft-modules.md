**Authors**: Jinghan Zhang, Shiqi Chen, Junteng Liu, Junxian He

**Venue**: NeurIPS 2023

This paper explores the composition of parameter-efficient fine-tuning (PEFT) modules using arithmetic operations. The approach enables flexible combination of task-specific adaptations in large language models without full retraining.
