**Authors**: Shiqi Chen, Miao Xiong, Junteng Liu, Zhengxuan Wu, Teng Xiao, Siyang Gao, Junxian He

**Venue**: ICML 2024

This paper proposes using in-context sharpness of inner representations as alerts for hallucination detection and mitigation in large language models. The approach leverages the inner representation perspective to identify and reduce hallucinations.
