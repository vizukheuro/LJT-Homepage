**Authors**: Junteng Liu, Yuanxiang Fan, Zhuo Jiang, Han Ding, Yongyi Hu, Chi Zhang, Yiqi Shi, Shitong Weng, Aili Chen, Shiqi Chen, Yunan Huang, Mozhi Zhang, Pengyu Zhao, Junjie Yan, Junxian He

**Venue**: Arxiv, 2025

**First author**

This paper introduces SynLogic, a framework for synthesizing verifiable reasoning data at scale to improve logical reasoning capabilities in large language models. The approach enables the generation of diverse, high-quality reasoning datasets that can be used to train and evaluate models on complex logical reasoning tasks.
