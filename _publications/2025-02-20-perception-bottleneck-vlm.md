**Authors**: Junteng Liu, Weihao Zeng, Xiwen Zhang, Yijun Wang, Zifei Shan, Junxian He

**Venue**: Arxiv, 2025

**First author**

This paper investigates the perception bottleneck in vision-language models (VLMs) when applied to chart understanding tasks. The work identifies key limitations in how VLMs process and interpret visual chart data, and proposes approaches to mitigate these bottlenecks.

Code repository: Vision4Chart
